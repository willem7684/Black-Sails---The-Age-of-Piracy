# Grace O'Malley

A legendary Irish pirate queen who commands respect both on land and at sea. Grace is a master navigator and strategist, leading her crew to victory with her sharp mind and fearless leadership.

## Strength

Tactical Genius - Grace's strategic mind and extensive knowledge of naval warfare enable her to outmaneuver and outwit her adversaries.

## Weakness

Stubbornness - Grace's stubbornness and reluctance to compromise can sometimes lead to conflicts with other pirates or authorities, hindering her ability to negotiate or form alliances.
